Welcome to my personal portfolio, my name is Joseph Fitzgerald. I am a Software Engineer passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems. I currently work as an Application Developer at The Metropolitan Transportation Authority in the State of New York. I am an engineering professional with a Bachelor's degree focused in Computer Engineering from Syracuse University. As an engineer, I am determined to make a difference in the lives of others utlizing my skills and expertise in a veriety of technologies.

I am actively working on several new projects. My current projects include a Spotify Song Data Visualizer, this website and learing Rust.
