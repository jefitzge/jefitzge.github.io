Hello, my name is Joseph Fitzgerald. I currently work as an Application Developer at The Metropolitan Transportation Authority in the State of New York. I am an engineering professional with a Bachelor's degree focused in Computer Engineering from Syracuse University. As an engineer, I am determined to make a difference in the lives of others using my skills and expertise. To deliver solutions I utilize my skills in DevSecOps, Agile development and a variety of programming languages.


My passion for programming and engineering started at a young age. I have always been curious about the technology around us. Taking apart anything I could get my hands on and trying to reassemble it to learn how it worked. Fascinated with how the world around me worked and my love for exploration I chose engineering. My time at Syracuse University has offered me a wealth of knowledge and a way to explore the world in a multitude of ways.
